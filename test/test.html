Welcome to the home of test matches!

Test match is the longest and oldest of the formats of cricket. It consists of four innings (two per team), lasts up to 5 days of play, and is played in all white clothing. A minimum of 90 overs are bowled each day. There are 12 countries that are recognized by the ICC as test countries: India, South Africa, Australia, England, New Zealand, West Indies, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh, Zimbabwe, Afghanistan, Ireland, and Pakistan.
